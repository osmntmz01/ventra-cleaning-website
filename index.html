The science

How we destroy odours.

Ozone (O₃) is nature's most powerful oxidiser. When it meets odour molecules, they break apart and disappear — permanently.

01

We place the generator

Our industrial ozone generator sits inside the vehicle or room, filling every cubic metre with ozone molecules that reach where sprays and shampoos never can.

02

Ozone oxidises the odour

O₃ molecules bond with odour compounds — bacteria, cigarette tar, pet urine proteins — and break them apart at the molecular level. No fragrance, no masking, just destruction.

03

We ventilate

After treatment, we open everything up. The ozone naturally converts back into O₂ (oxygen) within minutes. No residue, no chemicals, nothing to wipe down.

04

You enjoy the results

Step back in to a genuinely fresh space. If anything lingers, we come back — free. Our 100% guarantee isn't fine print, it's the whole point.
