FAQ

Common questions.

How does ozone actually eliminate odours?▼
Ozone (O₃) reacts with odour-causing compounds, oxidising and breaking down their chemical bonds permanently. Unlike sprays that mask, ozone destroys at the molecular level. It then converts back to oxygen — leaving no residue.
Is ozone treatment safe?▼
Yes — when handled by professionals. The space is unoccupied during treatment. Once complete, we ventilate thoroughly. Re-entry within 30–60 minutes. Strict safety protocols every job.
How long does it take?▼
Ozone treatment and spot cleaning: ~1 hour. Seat + floor: 2 hours. Severe odours may need extended treatment — we'll advise beforehand.
Do you guarantee the odour is gone?▼
100%. If any odour persists, we return at no extra cost. If you're not satisfied, we haven't finished the job.
Can you eliminate cigarette smoke in a car?▼
One of our most common treatments. Ozone penetrates upholstery, headliner, carpet, and AC system. Most heavy smoke cases — fully resolved in a single 1-hour session.
I'm an Uber driver. Same-day possible?▼
Absolutely. Drop off at our Melbourne facility — turnaround in 1–2 hours. Call 0432 066 372 for urgent jobs.
